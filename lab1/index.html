Adriano Celentano

PLOT

Adriano Celentano (ital. Adriano Celentano; born January 6, 1938, Milan , Kingdom of Italy) is an Italian singer, film actor , TV presenter , composer , director and public figure.


Celentano began his musical career in 1956, which continues to this day, and has released over forty studio albums during that time. One of the most successful and influential performers in the history of Italian music [ 1 ] [ 2 ] . He is considered one of the founders and leaders of Italian rock and roll [ 3 ] . Celentano is the owner of the recording studio "Clan Celentano" , which he created in 1961.


From 1958 to 1992, he starred in over forty films and directed four films and the 2019 animated series Adrian . Since the second half of the 1950s, Celentano has appeared on television as an invited guest, host and author of a TV show.


Winner of film awards: "Golden Globe" for the best acting debut in a male role in the film "Serafino" (1968), "Silver Ribbon" for the best soundtrack for the film "Yuppie-Doo" (1975), "David di Donatello" for the best male role in the films "Bluff" (1976) and "Velvet Hands" (1980). Winner of the Adriatic Festival of Light Music in Ancona (1959), the song contest "Cantajiro" (1962) and the Sanremo Festival (1970).


Since 1964, he has been married to the actress and singer Claudia Mori . Father of Rosita , Rosalind and Giacomo Celentano . In Italy, he got the nickname "Molleggiato" ("Boy on springs") for his way of moving on stage [ 4 ] .


My mother used to wake me up with rock and roll, which she adored, she would turn on the record and say: "Adriana, look, it's already eight o'clock!". Although in reality it was only seven
— Celentano
